## Core Concept

A frontend app should be built from smaller components.

This makes it easier to build manually or with AI tools.

Instead of asking AI to build the whole app, I can ask it to build:

- TodayPlanCard
- TomorrowPlanCard
- TripDashboard
- ChatAssistant
- ProposedChangeCard

## Why This Matters

Small component tasks reduce:

- Credit usage
- Confusion
- Rewrites
- Debugging difficulty
- Scope creep

## MVP Component Flow

The app should have:

```text
AppShell
Header
TodayPlanCard
TomorrowPlanCard
TripDashboard
FullItineraryList
ChatAssistant
PromptChips
ProposedChangeCard
VersionSummary
```

## Key Lesson

A big product becomes buildable when it is broken into clear components.
