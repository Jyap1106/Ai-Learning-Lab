## Core Concept

A good build prompt should tell the AI builder:

- What product it is building
- What files to read
- What version to build
- What features to include
- What features to exclude
- What success looks like

## Important Direction

The AI builder should understand the full product vision, but build only V1.

This keeps the build aligned without overbuilding.
