# Tokyo - 5 Days 4 Nights

## Trip Summary

Destination: Tokyo, Japan  
Duration: 5 days 4 nights  
Travel Style: Food, shopping, city exploration  
Budget: Medium  
Group Type: Couple or friends  

## Day 1

Morning:
- Arrive in Tokyo
- Check in or store luggage

Afternoon:
- Explore Shibuya
- Visit Shibuya Crossing
- Walk around shopping streets

Evening:
- Dinner near Shibuya
- Optional visit to an observation deck

Food:
- Ramen
- Izakaya-style dinner

Transport:
- Use train/subway

Notes:
- Keep Day 1 light because of arrival fatigue

## Day 2

Morning:
- Visit Asakusa
- Explore Senso-ji area

Afternoon:
- Walk around Ueno or Akihabara

Evening:
- Dinner near Ueno or Akihabara

Food:
- Sushi
- Street snacks

Transport:
- Use train/subway

Notes:
- Good day for culture and casual shopping

## General Tips

- Group nearby areas together
- Avoid overloading the first day
- Leave buffer time for transport
- Keep rainy-day alternatives
