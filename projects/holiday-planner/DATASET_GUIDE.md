# Dataset Guide: Holiday Planner

## Purpose

This guide explains how itinerary data should be prepared before it is used in the Holiday Planner project.

## Safe Data to Include

It is generally safe to include:

- Destination
- Trip duration
- General travel dates or season
- Activities
- Food places
- Area names
- Transport notes
- Budget estimates
- Travel tips
- Personal ratings or comments without private details

## Data to Remove

Remove:

- Passport details
- Booking reference numbers
- Flight booking codes
- Hotel confirmation numbers
- Full traveler names
- Phone numbers
- Emails
- Payment details
- Private personal notes
